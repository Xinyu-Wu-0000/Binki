权威英汉双解
n.
1.  [c]女裙；裙子  a piece of clothing for a woman or girl that hangs from the waist  
2.  [c]（连衣裙、外衣等的）下摆  the part of a dress, coat, etc. that hangs below the waist  
3.  [c]（车辆或机器基座的）挡板，裙板  an outer covering or part used to protect the base of a vehicle or machine  
v.
1.  [t]环绕…的四周；位于…的边缘；沿…的边缘走  to be or go around the edge of sth  
2.  [t]绕开，回避（话题）  to avoid talking about a subject, especially because it is difficult or embarrassing
